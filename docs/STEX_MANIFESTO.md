# STEX Manifesto

## Why STEX Exists

Artists should not have to become engineers to create manufacturable stencils.

Engineering software should adapt to artists—not force artists to adapt to engineering software.

STEX exists to bridge that gap.

It transforms artwork into manufacturable stencil designs while preserving the artist's intent.

The goal is not to automate creativity.

The goal is to remove technical barriers so creativity can flow.

---

## What Makes STEX Different

STEX is not another image editor.

STEX understands stencil topology.

It knows what a white island is.

It understands supports.

It understands strength.

It understands manufacturability.

---

## Our Promise

Every decision should answer one question:

Does this help someone create?

If the answer is no...

It doesn't belong in STEX.
