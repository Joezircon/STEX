# STEX Design Language

## Identity

Graffiti energy with a synthwave vibe.

## Colors

- Charcoal Black
- Neon Pink
- Cyan
- Electric Green
- Deep Purple
- Warning Orange

## Mascot

Ribot is the mascot.

Ribot should feel like a retro CRT robot from an arcade universe, but with graffiti/maker energy.

## UI Personality

Clean enough to work in for hours.  
Neon enough to feel alive.

## Branding

STEX should feel creative, physical, and bold.

Not vandalism. Creation.
