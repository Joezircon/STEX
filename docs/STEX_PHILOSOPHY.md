# STEX Philosophy

## Mission

STEX exists to transform artwork into manufacturable stencils while preserving the artist's original vision.

## Core Principles

### 1. Preserve the Artwork

Every change should respect the original design.

### 2. Artist First

The artist is always in control. STEX suggests. The artist decides.

### 3. Hide the Engineering

Supports should become part of the artwork whenever possible.

### 4. Zero White Islands

Unsupported white islands are defects. Black islands are acceptable.

### 5. Topology Before Cutting

Every design should be structurally sound before export.

### 6. Creativity Comes First

STEX exists to help people create.

### 7. Graffiti Energy

STEX should feel energetic, bold, and creative, inspired by synthwave, graffiti, 1980s arcade culture, comic books, and maker culture.

### 8. Human + AI

AI is an assistant. It should never silently alter artwork. Every significant change should be previewed and approved.
