# STEX Philosophy

## Mission

STEX exists to transform artwork into manufacturable stencils while preserving the artist's original vision.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Preserve the Artwork

Every change should respect the original design.

---

### 2. Artist First

The artist is always in control.

STEX suggests.
The artist decides.

---

### 3. Hide the Engineering

Supports should become part of the artwork whenever possible.

---

### 4. Zero White Islands

Unsupported white islands are defects.

Black islands are acceptable.

---

### 5. Topology Before Cutting

Every design should be structurally sound before export.

---

### 6. Creativity Comes First

STEX exists to help people create.

The software should never get in the way of creativity.

---

### 7. Graffiti Energy

STEX should feel energetic, bold, and creative.

Inspired by:

- Synthwave
- Graffiti
- 1980's arcade culture
- Comic books
- Maker culture

---

### 8. Human + AI

AI is an assistant.

It should never silently alter artwork.

Every significant change should be previewed and approved.

---

### 9. The Goal

Help artists create beautiful stencils that are both manufacturable and true to their original vision.
